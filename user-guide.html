The Trade Factory — EdgeOS Philosophy

read once · shapes how you use the system
Process over outcome.
Setups over stocks.
Price is the ultimate truth.

**Process over outcome.

Setups over stocks.

Price is the ultimate truth.**

The business of swing trading requires a reliable, repeatable production system. Without setups there is no product. Without product the business cannot run. The Trade Factory is that production system — running 24/7, 365 days a year, regardless of market conditions.

The operator does not manage by results. The operator manages by whether the factory is running correctly and whether the production lines are producing qualified output. Good trades come from good process. The outcome of any single trade is irrelevant to whether the process is sound.

Stocks are raw material. Setups are the product. The operator is never loyal to a stock — only to the setup it produced.

\

🟢 Bullish Setup Factory

Continuously produces qualified long trade ideas. Runs at all times — even in a bear market. A bullish setup appearing during a broad selloff is not noise. It is a signal worth investigating. Why is this stock going up when everything else is falling? Price always knows first.

🔴 Bearish Setup Factory

Continuously produces qualified short trade ideas. Also runs at all times. Market regime determines how much weight you assign to its output — not whether it runs.

Design Principles
One line per stage.Each production line maps to one stage label or stage transition. No sub-lines. Complexity lives in the tags — not in the line structure.
Tags are operator context.Each line carries tags that describe variants, conviction enhancers, nuances, and sub-patterns the operator uses for deep dive. Tags do not change whether a stock qualifies for the line — they enrich the operator's read of the output.
Thresholds are intelligent defaults.Where thresholds appear they are based on Weinstein methodology, validated chart examples, and trading logic. They are labeled as operator-configurable. When more proof accumulates the defaults get refined. Validated assumptions are better than no assumptions.
No cross-contamination.Lines never communicate with each other. The operator synthesizes. The lines produce independently.

Multi-Line Conviction

When a stock appears on multiple independent production lines at the same time, each line is validating the same setup from a different analytical framework. More lines agreeing = higher conviction = larger position justified.

Example: Three lines firing on the same stock
Stage 2A BreakoutStructure confirms — stock just exited its base
Momentum Count 1–4Momentum cycle just started — maximum runway ahead
SATY 4 Modes TriggeredAll four timeframes agree — highest trigger confirmation
Three independent systems agree → size up. Each line validates from a different angle. They never communicate with each other. The synthesis happens in your mind.
Cash Is a Valid Factory Output

When the production lines produce no qualified setups, cash is the correct output. This is not a failure state. The factory is working correctly — it is telling you that today's raw material does not meet production standards.

If there are no setups, there are no trades. Cash preserves capital for when the lines are running.
